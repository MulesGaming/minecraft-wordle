How to Play

To play the Minecraft Wordle you can use the on screen keyboard or computer keyboard to enter letters. When a letter is in the correct spot, it's this green color. When the letter is in the wrong spot but in the word it's this yellow color, and if the letter is not in the word at all, it's this gray color.
